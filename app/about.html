WHO IS WETLOOK?

Wetlook Hand Wash is an eco-friendly, hand car wash and detailing service based in Montreal. Our company was founded by a team of experts with more then 10 years of professional car wash experience. We operate three car washes throught Montreal area. Our goal is to provide our customers with the friendliest, most convenient hand car wash experience possible. We use the most modern and up-to-date water reclamation modules as a part of our car wash systems. Our products are all biodegradable and eco-friendly.
